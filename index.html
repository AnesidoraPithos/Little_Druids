Royal Charter of Habitation

Every home includes:

  • A living plant — the grounds themselves
  • A key character guardian: dragon, phoenix, gnome, frog, horse, duck, dove, snail, rabbit, or pig
  • A small forest sprite — a shy, dark guardian of the woodland, keeper of the roots
  • The dwelling itself: castle, garden house, or fairy house
  • A scattering of iridescent nacre
  • Tiny ladybugs
